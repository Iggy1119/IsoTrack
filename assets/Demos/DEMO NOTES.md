Helpful Tip: Perform against a wall to ensure stability!

__Anterior Deltoid Isometric__
Helpful Tip: Imagine performing a front raise, toward the sky!
Purpose: Train shoulder stability, and contribute to shoulder abduction.

__Ball Grip Isometric__
Helpful Tip: Think about driving your fingers toward the base of your palm!
Purpose: Train grip strength and distal forearm muscle endurance.

__Bicep Isometric__
Helpful Tip: Focus on the mind-muscle connection to the bicep!
Purpose: Train biceps to assist during pulling exercises and stability of arm. 

__Calf Raise Isometric__
Helpful Tip: Rise onto your toes, hold against a wall to ensure stability!
Purpose: Train stability during walking to minimize falls. 

__Cat Cow Lower Back Mobility__
Helpful Tip: Move slowly to focus on spinal flexion and extension!
Purpose: Train spinal mobility and reduce back stiffness.

__Door Pull Lats Isometric__
Helpful Tip: Imagine the movement of pulling a door!
Purpose: Train latissimus dorsi muscles for upper body stability and pulling strength. 

__Foot Dorsiflexion__ 
Helpful Tip: Imagine pulling your toes toward your forehead, keeping your heel planted. 
Purpose: Improve the tibialis anterior to minimize foot drop. 

__Forearm Flexion__
Helpful Tip: Rest your forearms on a table or your legs for stability!
Purpose: Prevent loss of grip-related strength. 

__Glute Bridge__
Helpful Tip: Squeeze your glutes at the top, avoid arching the lower back and keep the pelvis forward!
Purpose: Improve hip stability, glutes contribute to walking ability. 

__Hamstring Isometric__ 
Helpful Tip: Dig your heel into the seat and imagine bringing it to the floor. 
Purpose: Improve knee joint stability. 

__Hollow Body Holds__
Helpful Tip: Keep your lower back pressed into the ground.
Purpose: Improve core strength for stability.

__Lateral Deltoid Isometric__
Helpful Tip: Think about bringing your shoulder toward your ear in an arc.
Purpose: Prevent loss of shoulder abduction ability. 

__Pecs Isometric__
Helpful Tip: Imagine mind muscle connection and feel your pecs squeezing.
Purpose: Improve pecs for maintaining pushing abilities. 

__Rotator Cuff Internal Rotation__ 
Helpful Tip: Perform rotation slowly, to prevent damage. 
Purpose: Improve shoulder joint stability.

__Shrug Isometric__ 
Helpful Tip: Lift your shoulders toward your ear. 
Purpose: Prevents loss of grip strength, strengthens traps for neck/shoulder stability. 

__Tricep Kickback Isometric__
Helpful Tip: Imagine mind muscle connection and feeling your triceps contracting. 
Purpose: Improve elbow stability, prevent loss of pushing motion ability. 

__Wall Calf Stretch + Tibialis Hold__
Helpful Tip: Lift your toes on the front foot
Purpose: Strengthen Tibialis Anterior to minimize foot drop. 

__Wall Sits Quads__ 
Helpful Tip: Put a chair under you to prevent awkward failing positions. 
Purpose: Improve quadriceps endurance for staying mobile. 

__Wall Slides__
Helpful Tip: Keep as much of your arm in contact with the wall as possible. 
Purpose: Improve shoulder mobility.

__Wall Supported Lunge__
Helpful Tip: Use the wall for balance!
Purpose: Strengthen unilateral balance and stability.
