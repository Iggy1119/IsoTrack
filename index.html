Overview

A clearer, more supportive way to stay consistent with therapy.

IsoTrack helps patients complete guided therapeutic exercise with less friction, more structure, and clinician-aware reporting that supports long-term adherence.

Personalized daily Built around diagnosis, focus area, and current capacity.
Supportive guidance Clear exercise flow that helps patients know what to do next.
Clinician connected Useful summaries without showing discouraging metrics to patients.

How IsoTrack works

01. Start with a baseline

Set a profile using diagnosis, movement focus, and day-to-day ability.

02. Complete guided sessions

Follow a calmer, lower-load therapy flow designed for consistency and repeatability.

03. Share progress clearly

Turn adherence and session notes into clinician-ready summaries that support care.
